359,570 Downloads Last - Updated Apr 1, 2023 - Game Version 1.19.2

This mod is an addon to Create, it adds new variations of the Bogey aswell as expanding on them with new variations, rotations, colours and accents to help you build and enhance immersion in create.
For more decorations for your trains check out Dave's Building Extended Fabric or on Forge Powderlogy & Illumination, Dave's Building Extended

Extended Bogeys supports both 1.18.2 & 1.19.2 and create versions 0.5.0.h and above
